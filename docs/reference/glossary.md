# Glossary

Definitions of terms used throughout Arthalekha.

## A

### Account
A financial container where money is stored. Can be a bank account, wallet, credit card, investment account, or any place you track money. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/accounts)

### Account Type
The classification of an account (Cash, Savings, Credit Card, Wallet, Investment, Loan, Other). Determines how the account behaves and is displayed. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

## B

### Balance
The amount of money in an account. Can be positive (you have money) or negative (you owe money, as with credit cards). [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/balances)

### Biweekly
A frequency meaning every two weeks (14 days). Results in 26 occurrences per year. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)

## C

### Cash (Account Type)
An account type for physical currency - money in your wallet, at home, etc. Code: CA. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

### Credit Card (Account Type)
An account type for credit cards. Balance is typically negative (representing debt). Code: CC. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

### Creditor
In a transfer, the account receiving money (balance increases). Think of it as the account being "credited." [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/transfers)

### Current Balance
The real-time balance of an account, automatically updated with every transaction. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/balances)

## D

### Daily
A frequency meaning every day. Results in 365 occurrences per year. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)

### Debtor
In a transfer, the account sending money (balance decreases). Think of it as the account being "debited." [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/transfers)

## E

### Expense
Money spent from an account. Decreases the account balance. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/expenses)

### Export
Downloading transaction data as a CSV file for external analysis or backup. [Learn more →](/docs/features/exports)

## F

### Filter
A way to narrow down transaction lists by criteria like date, account, person, or tag. [Learn more →](/docs/features/filtering-and-search)

### Frequency
How often a recurring transaction repeats (Daily, Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly). [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)

## H

### Historical Balance
Monthly snapshots of account balances, allowing you to see how balances have changed over time. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/balances)

## I

### Income
Money received into an account. Increases the account balance. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/incomes)

### Initial Balance
The starting balance of an account on the date you begin tracking it. Forms the foundation for all balance calculations. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/accounts)

### Initial Date
The date from which you start tracking an account. All transactions should be on or after this date. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/accounts)

### Investment (Account Type)
An account type for investment holdings like stocks, mutual funds, or fixed deposits. Code: IN. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

## L

### Loan (Account Type)
An account type for loans taken (negative balance = debt) or loans given (positive balance = money owed to you). Code: LN. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

## M

### Monthly
A frequency meaning once per month. Results in 12 occurrences per year. The most common frequency for recurring transactions. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)

## N

### Net Worth
The total of all account balances (including negative balances from credit cards and loans). Represents your overall financial position.

## O

### Other (Account Type)
An account type for accounts that don't fit other categories. Code: OT. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

## P

### Person
A household or group member whose transactions you track separately. Helps understand individual spending patterns within a family. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/people)

### Projected Balance
Expected future account balance based on current balance plus scheduled recurring transactions. [Learn more →](/docs/features/projected-balance)

## Q

### Quarterly
A frequency meaning every three months. Results in 4 occurrences per year. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)

## R

### Recurring Expense
A template for expenses that happen regularly (rent, subscriptions, EMIs). Powers balance projections. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/recurring-transactions)

### Recurring Income
A template for income that arrives regularly (salary, rental income). Powers balance projections. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/recurring-transactions)

### Recurring Transaction
Any transaction template that repeats on a schedule. Includes recurring incomes, expenses, and transfers. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/recurring-transactions)

### Recurring Transfer
A template for transfers that happen regularly (monthly savings contributions). Powers balance projections. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/recurring-transactions)

### Remaining Recurrences
The number of times a recurring transaction will repeat before stopping. If not set, the transaction repeats indefinitely. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/recurring-transactions)

## S

### Savings (Account Type)
An account type for bank savings accounts, checking accounts, and current accounts. Code: SB. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

### Short Code
A two-letter abbreviation for account types (CA, SB, CC, WL, IN, LN, OT). Displayed in account labels for quick identification. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

## T

### Tag
A label attached to transactions for categorization. Enables filtering and analysis by category. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/tags)

### Transaction
Any financial event that affects an account balance. Can be an income, expense, or transfer.

### Transfer
Money moving between two accounts you own. Decreases one account and increases another; total net worth stays the same. [Learn more →](/docs/concepts/transfers)

## U

### User
The authenticated person using Arthalekha. All accounts and transactions belong to a user.

## W

### Wallet (Account Type)
An account type for digital wallets and payment apps (Google Pay, Paytm, etc.). Code: WL. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/account-types)

### Weekly
A frequency meaning every seven days. Results in 52 occurrences per year. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)

## Y

### Yearly
A frequency meaning once per year. Results in 1 occurrence per year. [Learn more →](/docs/reference/frequencies)
