# Introduction

**Arthalekha** (Sanskrit: अर्थलेखा, meaning "wealth record") is a comprehensive expense management application designed for families and groups to collaboratively track their finances.

## What is Arthalekha?

Arthalekha provides a complete financial tracking system that helps you:

- **Track multiple accounts** - Bank accounts, wallets, credit cards, investments, and more
- **Record transactions** - Log incomes, expenses, and transfers between accounts
- **Monitor balances** - Real-time balance tracking with historical records
- **Plan ahead** - Project future balances based on recurring transactions
- **Categorize with tags** - Flexible tagging system for organizing and analyzing spending
- **Collaborate** - Track spending by household or group members

## Key Features

### Multi-Account Management
Create and manage different types of accounts including savings accounts, credit cards, digital wallets, investment accounts, and cash.

### Transaction Tracking
Record every financial movement:
- **Incomes** - Money received into an account
- **Expenses** - Money spent from an account
- **Transfers** - Money moved between your accounts

### Recurring Transactions
Set up recurring incomes (like salaries), expenses (like subscriptions), and transfers to automate tracking and enable accurate future projections.

### Balance Projections
See projected account balances up to 12+ months in the future, helping you plan for upcoming expenses and financial goals.

### Family/Group Tracking
Associate transactions with specific people in your household or group to understand individual spending patterns. Use **Family Mode** to view consolidated financial data from all family members in a read-only format, perfect for monthly reviews and household financial planning.

### Flexible Categorization
Use tags to categorize transactions however makes sense for you - by category, project, or any other criteria.

## Quick Start

1. **Create your accounts** - Add your bank accounts, wallets, and other financial accounts
2. **Set initial balances** - Enter the starting balance for each account
3. **Start recording** - Log your daily incomes and expenses
4. **Add recurring items** - Set up recurring transactions for regular incomes and bills
5. **Analyze and plan** - Use the dashboard and projections to understand your finances

## Who is Arthalekha for?

Arthalekha is ideal for:

- **Families** wanting to track household expenses together
- **Individuals** managing multiple accounts and financial goals
- **Small groups** sharing expenses or managing joint finances
- **Anyone** who wants a clear picture of where their money goes
